Sharks

The Shark Epidemic

Shark finning is a popular practice that involves the removal of fins often while the shark is still alive. It is estimated that 100 million sharks are killed annually. This website is a petition to make a difference! Donations are also available.

Shark Facts

  • Sharks give live birth.
  • Sharks can cycle up to 35,000 teeth in their lifetimes.
  • Females are larger than males.
  • The Blue Shark can give birth to up to 135 pups in one litter!
  • Over 400 species of sharks!
  • You are 1,000 times more likely to drown at sea than to be attacked by a shark!
